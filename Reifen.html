Tyres

Tyres are now one of the most important components of a Formula 1 car. The grip, i.e. the adhesion of the tyres, significantly determines, for example, the cornering speed, which in turn influences the total time. The importance of good tyres is also evident in the fact that in 2006 on some circuits with many fast bends, such as the Circuit de Catalunya, the overall times increased only slightly compared to the previous year, despite the introduction of about 200 hp weaker V8 engines.
